## About

I am a professor and researcher at **FEA-USP** (Universidade de São Paulo), working at the intersection of financial econometrics, asset pricing, and macroeconomics. My research spans cryptocurrency markets, commodity futures, and Brazilian macroeconomic policy — with a focus on building rigorous empirical and theoretical frameworks for publication in top-tier journals.

---

## Research Interests

| Area | Topics |
|------|--------|
| 🪙 **Crypto & DeFi** | Stablecoin depegging, bubble detection, herding, market microstructure |
| 🌾 **Commodity Markets** | Futures term structure, position limits, hedging |
| 🇧🇷 **Brazilian Macro** | Fuel pricing policy, fiscal risk, exchange rate transmission |

---

## Selected Publications & Working Papers

- **Stablecoin Depegging and Currency Crisis Theory** — multiple equilibria framework with regime-dependent MLE *(working paper)*
- **CFTC Position Limits and Commodity Market Stability** — staggered DiD with exact randomization inference *(working paper)*
- **Cryptocurrency Herding around the 2024 U.S. Election** — synthetic DID, *Economics Letters* *(under review)*
- **Bitcoin Predictability across Market Regimes** — frequency-domain Granger causality & transfer entropy *(working paper)*
- Published in the **Journal of Futures Markets**
